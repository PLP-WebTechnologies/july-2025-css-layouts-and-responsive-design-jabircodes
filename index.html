History of Football

Modern football originated in England in the 19th century. The Football Association was founded in 1863.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 11 players
  • 90-minute match divided into two halves
  • Players cannot use hands (except goalkeepers)

Major Competitions

International

  • FIFA World Cup
  • UEFA European Championship

Club

  • UEFA Champions League
  • English Premier League

Did You Know?

Football is the most popular sport in the world, with over 4 billion fans globally.

The first FIFA World Cup was held in 1930 in Uruguay.
